How it works

From initial consultation to thriving research community in 4 weeks

  • Step 1
    Initial consultations
    Connect with our technical team to share your institutional needs, ask questions about federated infrastructure, and discuss deployment timeline. We'll assess your infrastructure, requirements, and integration needs.
  • Step 2
    Pilot agreement
    We prepare a tailored technical specification and implementation plan for any custom development, then agree on terms, timeline, and support scope.
  • Step 3
    Planning
    Collaborative session covering governance, moderation, onboarding strategy, and success metrics. We create a customised launch roadmap.
  • Step 4
    Pilot launch
    Immediate deployment of your pilot with support from our dedicated team.
  • Step 5
    Co-design & implementation
    We collaborate with you on the design of custom integrations or features, and implement them progressively, regularly deploying updates for rapid feedback and iteration.
  • Step 6
    Ongoing support
    Monthly check-ins, technical assistance, and support for your research community.
