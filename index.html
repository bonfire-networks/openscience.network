Deploy Your Research Community in Minutes

Industrial-grade federated infrastructure with no licensing fees.
Delivering the reliability, security, and compliance your research community needs.

Institutional SSO integration

ORCID certification and SSO integration with institutional identity providers (OAuth or OpenID connect). Researchers sign in once to verify and access their full professional profile.

GDPR compliant

Built to European data protection standards. Full data sovereignty, consent management, data portability and deletion, and privacy controls that meet institutional policies.

FAIR data

Social content become citable digital objects with persistent identifiers. Posts and discussions can be archived with DOIs and structured metadata, making research outputs discoverable and preserved in the scholarly record.

No fees

No licensing fees—ever. No per-seat pricing, no usage limits, no vendor lock-in. Fund tools and infrastructure for your institution and for the commons, not corporate profit.

Purpose-built for science

Publication previews, repository connectors, peer review workflows, and citation tracking. Every tool is designed for scientific collaboration from the ground up.

Network effects

Global collaboration, local autonomy. Researchers can connect across multiple networks while safeguarding data sovereignty.

Custom branding

Preserve your community's visual identity and create a place that feels like home with an online space that reflects your organisation, not the one-size-fits-all aesthetics of a corporate platform.

Custom roles

Flexible permissions to match your organisational structure. Configure roles for faculty, postdocs, students, and other collaborators with granular access and permission workflows.

Onboarding & support

Expert technical support available: onboarding, training, custom design and development, and ongoing support. We're with you every step.
